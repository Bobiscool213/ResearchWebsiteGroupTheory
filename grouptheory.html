The Mathematical Mysteries Underneath

There's so many mathematical secrets to be discovered between the arrays of this magic cube. From wooden blocks and glue to a cube that makes your brain scramble looking for solutions. To understand the Rubik's Cube in a mathematical sense, we will dig deep into the main group that brings all of its colorful arrays together. This is Group Theory.
